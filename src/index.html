BOYNEXTDOOR is a South Korean boy group formed under KOZ Entertainment, a subsidiary of HYBE Labels. They debuted on May 30, 2023, with their first single album Who! and gained attention for their relatable music and natural style. The group consists of six members: Sungho, Riwoo, Jaehyun (the leader), Taesan, Leehan, and Woonhak, with ages ranging from 17 to 21 years old. They aim to produce music that reflects the experiences of their generation, with tracks inspired by rock, hip-hop, synth-pop, and more.
